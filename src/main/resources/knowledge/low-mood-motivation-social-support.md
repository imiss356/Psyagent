# Risk Policy

Risk levels:

- LOW: Temporary mild low mood, occasional lack of motivation, simple complaints about little social interaction, no persistent negative thoughts.
- MEDIUM: Long-term continuous low mood, long-term loss of study/life motivation, long-term social isolation, frequent feelings of emptiness and loneliness, no self-harm plans.
- HIGH: Severe prolonged depression, complete loss of will to live, refuses all social contact and self-isolates, paired with explicit self-harm, suicidal thoughts or concrete harmful plans.

When risk is HIGH, the system records the report, writes it to the Excel ledger through the MCP-style tool, then sends an alert to counselors or administrators. The response should be empathetic and direct, validate the user’s painful feelings, and push them to reach out to trusted people and professional psychological help right away.

# 风险等级策略

风险等级包括 LOW、MEDIUM、HIGH。

- LOW 低风险：短暂心情低落、偶尔提不起劲、简单吐槽社交少，负面感受短暂，无持续性消极思维。
- MEDIUM 中风险：长期持续情绪低沉，长期丧失学习、生活动力，持续回避社交、独处封闭，时常空虚孤独，但无自伤、自杀相关想法。
- HIGH 高风险：重度持续抑郁，完全丧失生活意愿，彻底隔绝所有人、长期自我封闭，同时存在明确自伤、自杀念头或具体伤害计划。

当风险等级为 HIGH 时，系统应记录心理报告，写入 Excel 台账，并通过 MCP 工具发送 alert 预警给辅导员或管理员。回复共情且直白，认可用户当下痛苦的感受，引导其立刻联系身边信任的人，主动寻求专业心理援助。
