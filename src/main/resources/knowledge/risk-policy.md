# Risk Policy

Risk levels:

- LOW: normal chat, mild stress, ordinary anxiety, no self-harm signals.
- MEDIUM: persistent anxiety, insomnia, low mood, pressure, loneliness, emotional distress without immediate self-harm intent.
- HIGH: explicit self-harm, suicide, severe hopelessness, plans to hurt oneself or others, or language suggesting immediate danger.

When risk is HIGH, the system records the report, writes it to the Excel ledger through the MCP-style tool, then sends an alert to counselors or administrators. The response should be empathetic and direct, and should encourage immediate human support.

# 风险等级策略

风险等级包括 LOW、MEDIUM、HIGH。

- LOW 低风险：普通聊天、轻微压力、一般焦虑，没有自伤 self-harm、自杀 suicide 或伤害他人的信号。
- MEDIUM 中风险：持续焦虑、长期 insomnia 失眠、低落 low mood、压力、孤独、明显 emotional distress 情绪困扰，但没有明确、立即的自伤计划。
- HIGH 高风险：明确提到 self-harm 自伤、suicide 自杀、严重绝望、已经计划伤害自己或他人，或者语言显示 immediate danger 立即危险。

当风险等级为 HIGH 时，系统应记录心理报告，写入 Excel 台账，并通过 MCP 工具发送 alert 预警给辅导员或管理员。回复应保持共情、直接和稳定，并鼓励学生立即联系身边可信任的人或专业支持。
