# Risk Policy

Risk levels:

- LOW: Users only ask about general campus consultation locations, normal curiosity about school mental health services, no negative emotional descriptions.
- MEDIUM: User expresses confusion about how to seek help, has mild emotional troubles and wants to find campus support channels, mentions stress but no self-harm/harmful intent.
- HIGH: User urgently demands emergency campus psychological rescue, states they are in critical emotional crisis and need immediate on-campus counselor intervention, with clear self-harm or suicidal hints.

When risk is HIGH, the system records the report, writes it to the Excel ledger through the MCP-style tool, then sends an alert to counselors or administrators. The response should be empathetic and direct, list the school’s 24-hour psychological emergency contact information clearly, and encourage immediate on-site or telephone consultation with campus counselors.

# 风险等级策略

风险等级包括 LOW、MEDIUM、HIGH。

- LOW 低风险：仅单纯询问校内心理咨询地点、心理中心开放时间，只是普通了解校园心理服务，无任何负面情绪倾诉。
- MEDIUM 中风险：不清楚校内求助流程、有情绪困扰想要寻找校园支持渠道，倾诉压力、低落情绪，但不存在自伤、伤人相关想法。
- HIGH 高风险：紧急索要校园心理急救渠道，自述当下情绪濒临崩溃、需要辅导员 / 心理老师立刻介入，伴随明确自伤、自杀倾向与危机表述。

当风险等级为 HIGH 时，系统应记录心理报告，写入 Excel 台账，并通过 MCP 工具发送 alert 预警给辅导员或管理员。回复应共情温和、表述清晰，明确给出校内 24 小时心理危机热线、心理咨询室地址，引导学生立刻联系校内专业心理老师求助。
