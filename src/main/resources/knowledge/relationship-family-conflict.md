# Risk Policy

Risk levels:

- LOW: Minor temporary quarrels with friends or family, brief friction in daily communication, mild irritability without sustained emotional trauma.
- MEDIUM: Frequent long-term family/peer conflicts, constant verbal blame and cold violence, sustained sadness, resentment and helplessness, no self-harm or violent retaliation intent.
- HIGH: Severe violent conflicts with family or acquaintances, enduring long-term verbal/physical abuse at home, conflict triggers obvious self-harm, suicidal ideas or plans to fight back violently.

When risk is HIGH, the system records the report, writes it to the Excel ledger through the MCP-style tool, 
then sends an alert to counselors or administrators. The response should be empathetic and direct, acknowledge the pain brought by conflicts, and encourage the user to seek face-to-face mediation and psychological counseling from school staff without delay.

# 风险等级策略

风险等级包括 LOW、MEDIUM、HIGH。

- LOW 低风险：和亲友短暂小争执、日常沟通轻微摩擦，短暂烦躁，不会产生持续心理创伤。
- MEDIUM 中风险：长期频繁家庭、人际矛盾，持续遭受言语指责、冷暴力，长期压抑委屈、无助痛苦，但不存在自伤、暴力报复的想法。
- HIGH 高风险：和家人、熟人爆发严重肢体冲突，长期在家承受言语 / 肢体虐待，矛盾刺激下产生清晰自伤、自杀念头，或是计划采取暴力反击。

当风险等级为 HIGH 时，系统应记录心理报告，写入 Excel 台账，并通过 MCP 工具发送 alert 预警给辅导员或管理员。回复共情直白，接纳用户因冲突产生的痛苦情绪，督促其尽快找校内老师当面调解、接受专业心理疏导。
