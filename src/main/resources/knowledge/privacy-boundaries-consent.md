# Risk Policy

Risk levels:

- LOW: Normal discussion about personal space, general confusion about interpersonal boundary definition, no forced contact or privacy violation experience described.
- MEDIUM: User describes repeated invasion of personal privacy, long-term uncomfortable cross-boundary interactions, feels pressured by others’ excessive demands, no immediate threatening behavior.
- HIGH: User states being severely violated of privacy, enduring forced intimate contact, stalking, blackmail using private content, or receiving clear threats to personal safety.

When risk is HIGH, the system records the report, writes it to the Excel ledger through the MCP-style tool, then sends an alert to counselors or administrators. The response should be empathetic and direct, inform the user of ways to protect personal information, and advise contacting school staff or even legal support immediately.

# 风险等级策略

风险等级包括 LOW、MEDIUM、HIGH。

- LOW 低风险：正常探讨个人空间，单纯疑惑人际边界如何界定，未讲述被越界、隐私被侵犯的经历。
- MEDIUM 中风险：诉说自身隐私多次被打探侵犯，长期遭遇让人不适的越界行为，被他人过度索取个人信息、感到压迫，但无直接人身威胁行为。
- HIGH 高风险：遭受严重隐私泄露侵害，遭遇强制亲密接触、跟踪骚扰、被私人影像要挟，或是收到明确人身安全恐吓。

当风险等级为 HIGH 时，系统应记录心理报告，写入 Excel 台账，并通过 MCP 工具发送 alert 预警给辅导员或管理员。回复共情清晰，告知用户保护个人信息、隔离骚扰者的方法，劝导立刻联系校内管理人员，必要时寻求法律协助。
